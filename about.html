How can we lower the absence?

The absence percentage can and will never be perfect.
There is always going to be someone staying home because their injured or sick obviously.
But even tho it always will be someone away from class we have to work to get it better.
What we think is a good way to make the absence get lower
is to take notes on who is in class and who is not over a longer period,
then collect all information and scan it into a database.
Then look at who has the most absence and talk closer with them
to find out why they are gone from class.
After that you need to make a plan on how to motivate them and
how to get them wanting to go to school with a mentor of a kind.
